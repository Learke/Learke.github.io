Learke

I make songs and games!

I grew up in the suburbs just north of Brisbane, Australia, originally focusing only on games. Over the years, I've grown largely invested in music, and enjoy making it a lot! I'm currently 17, and have been working as an independent artist for the last four years now (my first single "inmyhead" came out on November 29th 2017!) and have finally felt like I'm getting somewhere. Over the last two to three years, through some experimentation, I've come to the conclusion that I am non-binary (he/they). Influenced by the works of Jon Bellion, Stephen, AJR and (more recently) Weezer, I try to make the best music that I can. My next album "Xernia" will release between June and August this year!
